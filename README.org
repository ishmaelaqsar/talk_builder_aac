* Talk Builder AAC - Sentence Building for Early Intervention

Talk Builder AAC is a Flutter application designed for children (ages 2-5) who are ready to transition from single words to building sentences.

Unlike general AAC apps that can be overwhelming, this app bridges the gap between simple "choice boards" and complex communication systems. It introduces the concept of a **Sentence Strip** while maintaining the **safety** and **simplicity** of the original /Toddler Talk/ philosophy[cite: 2].

** ðŸ“– Parent Guide

*** How to Edit Cards
1. Open the app.
2. Find the **Lock Icon** in the top right corner.
3. **Press and Hold** (Long Press) for 1 second until you see "Admin Mode"[cite: 14].
4. You will now see:
   - A "+" button to add new photos.
   - "Delete" and "Hide" icons on custom cards.
   - A "Settings" gear for Backup/Restore[cite: 16].

*** How to Import from "Toddler Talk"
1. In your old *Toddler Talk* app, go to Settings -> **Backup Data** and save the ~.zip~ file.
2. Open *Talk Builder*.
3. Enter **Admin Mode** (Long Press Lock).
4. Tap the **Settings Gear** and select **Import from Toddler Talk**.
5. Select the ~.zip~ file you created. Your photos will appear automatically!
